======= v2.0 ==========
General Stuff
    Zoom
        Given how zooming in makes nearby mobs not tick, the max zoom is being reduced a bit
        - Reduced max zoom (3 -> 2)

Leaders
    Right now leaders are absolute tanks. While this is mostly due to low enemy damage numbers,
    reducing the health to a power of 10 makes knowing how much damage it does to a leader easier.

    Furthermore, leaders are noticably a bit slow.

    - Health Decreased (2000 -> 1000)
    - Movement speed increased (130 -> 150)

Pikmin
    This makes sure that pikmin aren't instantly killed by higher power attacks, and otherwise doesn't affect gameplay
    The increased health regen makes sure that they won't die to multiple repeated attacks
    - Health increased (100 -> 500)
    - Health regen increased (10 -> 100)
    - Move speed increased (110 -> 130)

    Despite this decrease, Red pikmin getting their 1.5x attack back means they are technically buffed (14.4 -> 15)
    - Maturity power mult decreased (0.1 -> 0)

    Red Pikmin:
        Red pikmin don't have their 1.5x attack damage... Why?
        - Damage increased (12 -> 15)
    Purple Pikmin:
        - Damage increased (11 -> 15)
        - Landing damage increased (8 -> 20)
        - Move speed increased (90 -> 105)
    Rock Pikmin:
        - Damage increased (20 -> 40)
    White Pikmin:
        - Speed increased (125 -> 150)

Statuses:
    Note: All hazard statuses need a slight health drain increase to account for the change in pikmin health regen (10%/sec -> 20%/sec)
    Furthermore, non liquid hazards are getting a slight buff to make them a bit more threatening

    Drowning
        -Health change decreased (-0.3 -> -0.4) (Net change: -20%/sec -> -20%/sec)

    These hazards are getting buffs in various ways to add some variety, and a bit of a heirarchy.
    Currently, fatal electricity is the most lethal, and the rest are basically equal.
    Bubbled
        I'm like 90% sure this is currently unused. Nevertheless, water is already super prominent, so this doesnt really need much of a buff.
        - Health change decreased (-0.3 -> -0.45) (Net change: -20%/sec -> -25%/sec)
    Burned
        Fire is currently not very prominent, and not very scary. This brings back some of that Pikmin 1 fear.
        - Health change decreased (-0.3 -> -0.7) (Net change: -20%/sec -> -50%/sec)
        - Maturity change amount decreased (-1 -> -2)
    Poisoned
        Poison is a bit more prominent than fire; gas pipes act like walls until they are destroyed.
        Instead of just a straight damage buff, they are also getting a speed increase to make whistling them slightly harder.
        - Health change decreased (-0.3 -> -0.45) (Net change: -20%/sec -> -25%/sec)
        - Speed multiplier increased (2 -> 3)

    Bomb rock health drain
        I think bombrocks having a shorter fuse incrases their risk and reward.
        On the one hand, you dont need to time them as well on mobile enemies
        On the other... if an enemy makes you drop one, well good luck.
        - Health change decreased (-0.25 -> -0.33)

    Petrified
        Currently bitter sprays are incredibly overpowered. This reworks them into less of an instant win, and more of a regroup option.
        - Duration reduced (10 -> 5)
        - Defense multiplier increased (1.2 -> 5)
    Petrified
        On the other hand, spicy sprays do nothing for combat. The logical option is an animation speed increase instead of a damage buff.
        - Animation speed increased (1 -> 1.25)

    Red Bulborb
        Red Bulborbs are weak when attacking from behind, and should pose more of a threat when approached from the front
        Buffs
            - Wake up reach now has a 50 unit range with 90 degrees, this should have them wake up when next to them from the front.
            - Increased full circle search reach (30 -> 40)
            - Decreased wake up time (1.35s -> 1.05s)
            - Decreased eating time (1.85s -> 1.4s)
            - Decreased shake wind up (0.7s -> 0.5s)
            - Legs now have a 80% damage reduction
            - Gained 25% damage reduction to bombrocks
        Nerfs
            - Health decreased (750 -> 700)
            - Failing length increased (0.7s -> 0.9s)
            - Decreased rotation speed (114 -> 104)
    Whiptongue Bulborb
        These guys are already quite strong compared to a red bulborb.
        For consistency, they are getting leg hitboxes as well.

        Buffs
            - Legs now have a 75% damage reduction.
            - Gained 25% damage reduction to bombrocks

    Fiery Blowhog

        Nerfs
